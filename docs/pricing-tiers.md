# Suggested Pricing Tiers

## Tier 1: Starter Lead List

Price range:

- $75 to $150

Includes:

- one niche
- one city or region
- up to 100 leads
- CSV export
- basic fields: business name, phone, website, directory URL

Best for:

- first-time clients
- quick validation projects
- simple outbound campaigns

## Tier 2: Standard Prospect Build

Price range:

- $200 to $400

Includes:

- one niche
- multiple cities or a broader metro area
- up to 300 leads
- deduplication
- optional email enrichment where available
- clean CSV delivery

Best for:

- agencies
- recruiters
- local business outreach

## Tier 3: Custom Lead Automation

Price range:

- $500 to $1,500+

Includes:

- custom scraper setup
- multi-source research
- larger lead volume
- enrichment and cleanup
- workflow documentation
- optional repeat runs or handoff

Best for:

- teams that need recurring lead generation
- niche market research
- custom prospecting workflows

## Positioning Notes

- Start with a low-friction offer so buyers can say yes quickly.
- Use custom quotes when the source site is hard to scrape or the enrichment requirements are heavier.
- Sell the outcome, not the scraper: "qualified lead list" is easier to buy than "Python script."

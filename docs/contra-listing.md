## Product Title

Custom Lead Scraper for Local Businesses and B2B Prospecting

## Short Pitch

I build custom lead scraping workflows that turn public business directories into clean, usable prospect lists for outreach, recruiting, and market research.

## Description

Need targeted business leads by niche and location?

I can build a custom lead scraper that collects public business listing data and exports it into a clean CSV your team can actually use.

This is a good fit for:

- agencies building outbound lists
- founders validating new markets
- recruiters sourcing niche companies
- local service businesses researching competitors and prospects
- sales teams that need fast list generation without manual copy-paste

Typical output includes:

- business name
- phone number
- website
- directory page URL
- contact email when available

You will receive a structured CSV plus a repeatable workflow that can be customized for your niche, location, and target websites.

Public directories, listing pages, and niche business websites, depending on the site's structure and access restrictions.

Usually CSV, but I can also prepare exports for Google Sheets, Airtable, or CRM import.

### Can you enrich the data?

Yes. I can often add website, phone, and contact email fields when those are publicly available.
